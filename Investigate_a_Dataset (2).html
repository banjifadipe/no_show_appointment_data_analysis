Conclusions¶

  1. Scholarship, Alcoholism and Received_SMS do not affect the appearance of patients at their appointments
  2. Older patients showed up for their appointments

What factors are important for us to know in order to predict if a patient will show up for their scheduled appointment?¶

Factors include:

  1. Age
  2. Type of disease excluding Alcoholism

Limitations¶

  • One major limitation to the analysis is the fact that not enough features or factors are considered in determining whether patients in Brazil will show up for their appointments. For example, factors like location could not be considered as the data did not capture all parts of Brazil. Furthermore, all the diseases mentioned in the data do not include every illness that may be common to the populace of Brazil. In all, this data is not robust enough to determine whether or not the patients of Brazil will show up for their hospital/doctor's appointments.
